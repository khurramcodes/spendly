Privacy Policy

Last updated: May 2026

1. Data We Collect

When you create an account, we collect your name and email address. When you use the Service, we store the expense records you enter, including amounts, categories, dates, and any descriptions you provide.

We do not collect payment information, government IDs, or any financial account credentials.

2. How We Use Your Data

Your data is used solely to provide and improve the Spendly Service. Specifically, we use it to display your expense history, generate summaries, and personalise your experience within the app.

We will never sell, rent, or share your personal data with advertisers or unaffiliated third parties for their own marketing purposes.

3. Data Storage

Your data is stored on secure servers. We take reasonable technical and organisational precautions to protect your information against loss, misuse, or unauthorised access.

You may request deletion of your account and all associated data at any time by contacting us. Deletion requests are processed within 30 days.

4. Third Party Services

Spendly does not currently integrate with any third-party analytics, advertising, or data-sharing services. If this changes, this policy will be updated and you will be notified.

Our application may use third-party fonts or libraries delivered via CDN. These providers may log standard request metadata such as your IP address in accordance with their own privacy policies.

5. Contact Us

If you have questions or concerns about this Privacy Policy or how your data is handled, please reach out to us at privacy@spendly.app.

We will respond to all privacy-related enquiries within 7 business days.
